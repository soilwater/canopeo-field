Guidelines
📷 Camera position
  • Hold the phone parallel to the ground, and frame the crop canopy pointing straight down
  • Keep it between waist and shoulder height (~0.8–1.5 m)
  • Avoid tilting — even a small angle reduces accuracy
🚫 What to avoid
  • Blurry images — hold still before tapping
  • Feet, tools, or other objects in the frame
  • Heavy shadows cast by yourself or equipment
  • Avoid low sun angles (early morning, late afternoon)
📍 Location & data
  • GPS coordinates and timestamp are recorded automatically
  • Wait for the GPS indicator to turn green before capturing
  • Images are saved locally on your device — upload is optional
📲 Offline use
  • Canopeo-Field works fully offline once loaded
  • On iPhone: tap Share → Add to Home Screen
  • On Android: tap the browser menu → Install app
